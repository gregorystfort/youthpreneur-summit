What is Youthpreneur?

The Youthpreneur Program is a dynamic, hands-on curriculum designed to spark creativity, build self-confidence, and introduce young people to real career pathways — with a strong focus on entrepreneurship.

Over the course of 6 weeks with twice-weekly sessions, students engage in interactive workshops, group discussions, and personal reflection exercises that unlock their potential and develop crucial transferable skills.

Whether you dream of launching a business, leading a team, or simply finding your voice — Youthpreneur is where it begins.

Who It's For: Teens ages 13–18 from all backgrounds, with a focus on empowering youth in underserved communities across Queens, NY and beyond.

A program by The Showing Hearts Foundation — Empowering communities for over 10 years.

Five Pillars. One Foundation.

💡

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Cultivate creativity, critical thinking, problem-solving, and resilience. Learn to see opportunity everywhere and turn ideas into action.

💰

Financial Literacy

Master basic financial concepts, personal budgeting, and business finance. Build the money skills that last a lifetime.

🧭

Career Exploration

Explore a wide variety of career paths and entrepreneurial opportunities. Hear from mentors who've walked the path.

🎤

Leadership & Communication

Develop public speaking, teamwork, and leadership abilities. Find your voice and learn to inspire others.

🚀

Hands-On Business Planning

Apply everything you've learned through real-life business planning and pitching. Present your ideas to a panel — just like the real thing.
